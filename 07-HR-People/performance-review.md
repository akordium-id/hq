## ðŸ“‹ Tujuan dan Overview

### **Tujuan Performance Review:**

- Menilai kontribusi karyawan terhadap tujuan perusahaan
- Memberikan feedback konstruktif untuk pengembangan karir
- Identifikasi kekuatan dan area untuk improvement
- Menentukan bonus, promosi, dan pengembangan karir
- Menciptakan culture of continuous feedback dan improvement

### **Review Cycle:**

- **Quarterly Review:** Informal check-in setiap 3 bulan
- **Annual Review:** Comprehensive evaluation tahunan
- **360-Degree Feedback:** Feedback dari peers, manager, dan direct reports (untuk senior roles)
- **Ad Hoc Review:** Review untuk performance improvement plans

### **Participants:**

- **Employee:** Self-assessment dan preparation
- **Manager:** Performance evaluation dan feedback
- **Peers:** Peer feedback (untuk 360-degree)
- **Direct Reports:** Upward feedback (untuk managers)
- **HR:** Process facilitation dan documentation

## ðŸŽ¯ Performance Evaluation Framework

### **Core Competencies (60%)**

#### **1. Technical Excellence (25%)**

- **Code Quality:** Clean, maintainable, dan efficient code
- **Problem Solving:** Analytical thinking dan creative solutions
- **Technical Knowledge:** Current dengan teknologi stack
- **Best Practices:** Following coding standards dan best practices
- **Innovation:** Contribute to process improvement

#### **2. Delivery & Execution (25%)**

- **Meeting Deadlines:** Consistent timely delivery
- **Quality Output:** High-quality deliverables
- **Project Management:** Effective task management
- **Initiative:** Proactive problem identification
- **Accountability:** Take ownership untuk deliverables

#### **3. Collaboration & Communication (10%)**

- **Teamwork:** Effective collaboration dengan team members
- **Communication:** Clear dan professional communication
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Contribute ke team learning
- **Client Communication:** Professional client interaction
- **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Collaboration dengan other teams

### **Leadership Competencies (40%) - Untuk Leadership Roles**

#### **4. Team Leadership (20%)**

- **Vision & Strategy:** Ability to set clear direction
- **Team Development:** Mentoring dan team growth
- **Decision Making:** Timely dan informed decisions
- **Conflict Resolution:** Handle conflicts effectively
- **Performance Management:** Set clear expectations

#### **5. Business Impact (20%)**

- **Strategic Thinking:** Align dengan business goals
- **Innovation:** Drive process innovation
- **Client Satisfaction:** Maintain high client satisfaction
- **Financial Impact:** Contribute to business metrics
- **Risk Management:** Identify dan mitigate risks

### **Development Competencies (Untuk Junior Roles)**

#### **Learning & Growth (40%)**

- **Learning Speed:** Quick learning dari feedback
- **Skill Development:** Active skill improvement
- **Problem Solving:** Growing problem-solving capabilities
- **Initiative:** Proactive learning dan exploration
- **Adaptability:** Flexibility dengan changing requirements
