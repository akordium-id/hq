## üìã Sales Deck Framework Overview

### Strategic Purpose

**Primary Objectives:**

1. **Problem Awareness:** Establish clear understanding dari client business challenges
2. **Solution Validation:** Demonstrate Akordium's capability to solve specific problems
3. **Trust Building:** Build credibility melalui expertise, case studies, and social proof
4. **Value Communication:** Clearly articulate ROI dan business value
5. **Action Motivation:** Drive prospects toward specific next steps

### Audience Adaptation

#### Executive Audience (C-Level)

**Focus:** Business impact, ROI, competitive advantage, strategic alignment
**Content Emphasis:** High-level business metrics, strategic benefits, market positioning
**Tone:** Strategic, consultative, focused pada business outcomes

#### Technical Audience (CTO/Engineering)

**Focus:** Technical capabilities, architecture, integration, security, scalability
**Content Emphasis:** Technical architecture, implementation approach, system performance
**Tone:** Technical, detailed, focused pada solution quality and innovation

#### Operational Audience (Operations/Product)

**Focus:** User experience, implementation, training, support, adoption
**Content Emphasis:** Day-to-day operations, user experience, change management
**Tone:** Practical, process-oriented, focused pada operational efficiency

#### Financial Audience (CFO/Finance)

**Focus:** Cost-benefit analysis, ROI, TCO, payment structure, risk mitigation
**Content Emphasis:** Financial projections, cost savings, revenue impact, pricing
**Tone:** Data-driven, analytical, focused pada financial returns

## üé® Design Guidelines

### Visual Standards

#### Color Usage

- **Primary Colors:** Akordium Deep Blue (#1A365D) dan Tech Green (#22C55E)
- **Accent Colors:** Consistent dengan brand guidelines
- **Text Colors:** High contrast untuk readability
- **Background:** Clean white atau light gray untuk professionalism

#### Typography

- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk, bold, consistent sizing
- **Body Text:** Inter, regular, minimum 24pt untuk readability
- **Technical Text:** JetBrains Mono untuk code elements
- **Emphasis:** Bold atau color untuk key points

#### Layout Principles

- **Consistent Margins:** Standardized spacing throughout
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Clear information hierarchy
- **White Space:** Adequate breathing room untuk clarity
- **Grid System:** 12-column grid untuk alignment

### Content Guidelines

#### Slide Content Rules

- **One Idea Per Slide:** Focus on single concept per slide
- **Limited Text:** Maximum 6 bullet points per slide
- **Visual Balance:** Mix of text, visuals, and white space
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Build complexity gradually

#### Data Visualization

- **Clear Labels:** All charts and graphs clearly labeled
- **Consistent Colors:** Use consistent color coding for data
- **Scale Accuracy:** Ensure accurate representation of data
- **Source Attribution:** Cite data sources when applicable

#### Image Usage

- **High Quality:** Professional, high-resolution images only
- **Relevance:** Images must support slide message
- **Consistency:** Consistent style throughout presentation
- **Accessibility:** Alt text for all images
