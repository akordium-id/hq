### Content Creation Guidelines

#### Content Quality Standards

**Technical Content:**

- **Accuracy:** All technical information verified by subject matter experts
- **Clarity:** Complex concepts explained clearly dengan examples
- **Value:** Actionable insights that readers can implement
- **Depth:** Sufficient detail without overwhelming audience

**Visual Content:**

- **Quality:** High-resolution, professionally designed
- **Consistency:** Brand-aligned colors, typography, and style
- **Accessibility:** Alt text, readable text, proper contrast ratios
- **Relevance:** Visuals enhance, don't distract dari message

**Copywriting:**

- **Voice:** Consistent dengan "Technical Partner yang Cerdas & Pragmatis" persona
- **Clarity:** Clear, concise, scannable formatting
- **Engagement:** Questions, calls-to-action, discussion prompts
- **Localization:** Bahasa Indonesia dengan English technical terms

#### Approval Workflow

**Content Creation Process:**

1. **Idea Submission:** Team members submit content ideas via shared document
2. **Content Planning:** Social media manager reviews and approves ideas
3. **Content Creation:** Assigned creators produce content following guidelines
4. **Internal Review:** Subject matter experts review technical accuracy
5. **Brand Review:** Marketing team ensures brand consistency
6. **Final Approval:** Head of Marketing or CEO approves high-impact content
7. **Scheduling:** Content scheduled using social media management tools

**Emergency Content Protocol:**

- **Urgent Announcements:** CEO or Head of Marketing can approve immediately
- **Crisis Communication:** Crisis response team approves within 1 hour
- **Market Opportunities:** Marketing team can capitalize on timely opportunities

## ðŸŽ¯ Crisis Management & Brand Protection

### Crisis Response Protocol

#### Immediate Response (0-2 Hours)

**Crisis Identification:**

- **Technical Crisis:** Product outage, security breach, data issues
- **Brand Crisis:** Negative PR, customer complaints, media coverage
- **Team Crisis:** Employee issues, internal problems affecting brand
- **Competitive Crisis:** Market changes, competitive attacks

**Response Team Activation:**

1. **Crisis Lead:** CEO or Head of Marketing coordinates response
2. **Technical Expert:** CTO provides technical information
3. **Communications:** Marketing team handles external communications
4. **Legal:** Legal counsel reviews statements if necessary

**Initial Response Steps:**

- **Acknowledge:** Acknowledge issue quickly dan transparently
- **Assess:** Gather information, understand scope and impact
- **Communicate:** Provide initial statement, outline next steps
- **Monitor:** Track sentiment, respond to concerns

#### Ongoing Management (2-24 Hours)

**Regular Updates:**

- **Frequency:** Update every 2-4 hours depending on severity
- **Channels:** Primary platforms where crisis is being discussed
- **Content:** Progress updates, timeline for resolution, customer impact
- **Tone:** Transparent, empathetic, professional

**Community Management:**

- **Monitoring:** Track all mentions, comments, discussions
- **Response:** Reply to concerns, correct misinformation, provide support
- **Escalation:** Flag serious issues to appropriate team members
- **Documentation:** Log all interactions, track sentiment trends

#### Post-Crisis Analysis (24-48 Hours)

**Review Process:**

- **Root Cause Analysis:** Understand what happened and why
- **Response Evaluation:** Assess effectiveness of crisis communication
- **Impact Assessment:** Evaluate brand and business impact
- **Lessons Learned:** Document improvements for future incidents

**Recovery Communication:**

- **Resolution Announcement:** Clearly communicate problem resolution
- **Apology/Thank You:** Thank community for patience and support
- **Future Prevention:** Outline steps to prevent similar issues
- **Rebuilding Trust:** Demonstrate commitment to improvement

### Brand Protection Guidelines

#### Proactive Measures

**Content Guidelines:**

- **Fact-Checking:** Verify all technical claims and statistics
- **Source Attribution:** Credit sources, avoid plagiarism
- **Professional Language:** Maintain professional tone in all communications
- **Legal Compliance:** Ensure all content complies with regulations

**Security Protocols:**

- **Account Security:** Two-factor authentication, strong passwords
- **Access Control:** Limit posting access to authorized team members
- **Content Review:** Review process for all public content
- **Monitoring Tools:** Use social media monitoring for brand mentions

**Community Guidelines:**

- **Engagement Rules:** Clear guidelines for community interactions
- **Moderation Protocol:** Process for handling inappropriate content
- **Spam Prevention:** Measures to prevent and remove spam
- **Feedback Channels:** Clear channels for customer feedback and support

#### Reactive Measures

**Negative Feedback Handling:**

- **Acknowledge Quickly:** Respond within 2 hours during business hours
- **Empathize:** Show understanding of customer concerns
- **Solve:** Provide solutions or clear path to resolution
- **Follow Up:** Ensure issues are resolved satisfactorily

**Misinformation Correction:**

- **Identify:** Monitor for false information about the brand
- **Correct:** Provide accurate information politely and professionally
- **Educate:** Share accurate information through official channels
- **Report:** Report harmful misinformation to platform moderators

**Competitor Interactions:**

- **Professional Conduct:** Maintain professional tone in all interactions
- **Focus on Value:** Highlight own strengths rather than competitor weaknesses
- **Market Education:** Educate market about solutions rather than attacking competitors
- **Collaboration Opportunities:** Look for partnership opportunities where appropriate
