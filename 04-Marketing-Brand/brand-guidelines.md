- ‚ùå Change logo colors (kecuali approved variations)
- ‚ùå Add drop shadows, outlines, atau effects
- ‚ùå Rotate atau skew logo
- ‚ùå Place logo pada busy atau patterned backgrounds

## üñºÔ∏è Design Principles

### Core Design Principles

#### 1. Clarity First

- **Principle:** Information should be immediately understandable
- **Implementation:** Clear typography, ample white space, logical grouping
- **Example:** Use visual hierarchy, avoid clutter, maintain consistency

#### 2. Technical Precision

- **Principle:** Reflect technical expertise through attention to detail
- **Implementation:** Pixel-perfect alignment, consistent spacing, proper contrast ratios
- **Example:** Grid-based layouts, mathematical proportions, systematic scaling

#### 3. Accessibility Excellence

- **Principle:** Design untuk everyone, including users dengan disabilities
- **Implementation:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance minimum, AAA where possible
- **Example:** 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum, keyboard navigation, screen reader support

#### 4. Responsive by Default

- **Principle:** Experience should be excellent across all devices
- **Implementation:** Mobile-first approach, fluid typography, flexible layouts
- **Example:** Progressive enhancement, touch-friendly targets, optimized performance

## üìä Brand Voice Guidelines

### Communication Style

#### Core Communication Principles

**1. Technical Authority**

- Speak dengan confidence and expertise
- Use precise terminology, explain complex concepts simply
- Demonstrate deep understanding of technology and business
- Provide data-driven insights and evidence-based recommendations

**2. Pragmatic Problem-Solving**

- Focus pada practical solutions and tangible results
- Acknowledge challenges, provide realistic solutions
- Prioritize functionality over complexity
- Deliver clear, actionable advice

**3. Approachable Partnership**

- Use inclusive language ("we", "our", "together")
- Welcome questions and collaboration
- Maintain friendly, professional tone
- Show genuine interest in client success

#### Language Style Examples

**‚úÖ Correct Usage:**

- "Kami recommend menggunakan architecture microservices untuk scalability"
- "Based pada data dari 50+ clients, solusi ini meningkatkan efficiency 40%"
- "Let's optimize database query performance together"
- "System kami designed dengan security dan scalability sebagai priority"

**‚ùå Incorrect Usage:**

- "Menurut aku, ini adalah cara terbaik..." (Too informal)
- "Kami adalah ahli nomor satu di Indonesia..." (Overconfident)
- "Product kami adalah yang paling bagus" (Unsubstantiated claim)
- "Sistem kami gampang sekali dipakai" (Oversimplified)
