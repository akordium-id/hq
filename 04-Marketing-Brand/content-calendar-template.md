## üéØ Content Strategy Framework

### Strategic Content Pillars

#### Primary Content Pillars (70% dari total content)

**1. Technical Expertise (25%)**

- **Focus:** Deep technical knowledge, architecture patterns, best practices
- **Target Audience:** Technical decision makers, developers, engineering teams
- **Content Types:** Technical articles, code tutorials, architecture deep dives
- **Platforms:** LinkedIn (long-form), Twitter (quick tips), YouTube (tutorials)

**2. Product Excellence (20%)**

- **Focus:** Product features, case studies, implementation guides
- **Target Audience:** Current customers, potential buyers, product managers
- **Content Types:** Product demos, customer success stories, feature announcements
- **Platforms:** All platforms, dengan format customization

**3. Industry Insights (15%)**

- **Focus:** Market analysis, tech trends, digital transformation
- **Target Audience:** Business leaders, industry professionals, tech enthusiasts
- **Content Types:** Industry analysis, trend predictions, market research
- **Platforms:** LinkedIn (professional insights), Twitter (quick takes)

**4. Company Culture (10%)**

- **Focus:** Team stories, company values, work environment
- **Target Audience:** Potential employees, partners, community
- **Content Types:** Team profiles, behind-the-scenes, company milestones
- **Platforms:** Instagram (visual), Facebook (community), LinkedIn (professional)

#### Secondary Content Pillars (30% dari total content)

**5. Educational Content (15%)**

- **Focus:** Technical education, skill development, career guidance
- **Target Audience:** Students, junior developers, career changers
- **Content Types:** How-to guides, career advice, technical tutorials
- **Platforms:** YouTube (in-depth), Instagram (quick tips), Twitter (threads)

**6. Community Engagement (15%)**

- **Focus:** Community building, interactions, user-generated content
- **Target Audience:** Tech community, followers, industry peers
- **Content Types:** Q&A sessions, community highlights, user stories
- **Platforms:** All platforms dengan interactive focus
