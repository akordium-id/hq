## ğŸ¬ Content Production Workflow

Detailed workflow ada di [04-Workflow-Checklist](./04-workflow-checklist.md), but at high level:

1. **Project Selection** (1 jam)
   - Pilih case study paling compelling
   - Confirm client approval untuk publikasi
   - Assign content lead

2. **Master Documentation** (15-20 jam, Week 1-2)
   - Kumpulkan raw materials dari team
   - Write comprehensive case study
   - Build visual asset library
   - Internal review & approval

3. **Atomization Planning** (4-6 jam, Week 2)
   - Identify goldmine moments
   - Map content to platforms
   - Create 3-6 month content calendar

4. **Batch Content Creation** (20-30 jam, Week 3-4)
   - Batch 1: Written content
   - Batch 2: Visual content
   - Batch 3: Video content
   - Batch 4: Short-form video

5. **Distribution Setup** (2-3 jam, Week 4)
   - Schedule posts across platforms
   - Set up tracking & analytics
   - Brief team on engagement strategy

6. **Ongoing Optimization** (1-2 jam/week)
   - Monitor performance
   - Engage with comments
   - Repurpose top performers

## ğŸ™‹ FAQs

**Q: Apakah kita harus publish di semua platform?**  
A: Tidak! Start dengan 2-3 platform yang paling strategic (recommend: LinkedIn + Instagram + Blog). Scale gradually.

**Q: Berapa lama untuk create 1 master documentation?**  
A: 15-20 jam total, bisa split across team members (PM, designer, developer).

**Q: Bagaimana jika client tidak approve publikasi?**  
A: Anonymize details, atau gunakan internal projects (Anggarin, WaqfWise, Atribuo) sebagai case study.

**Q: Siapa yang responsible untuk content creation?**  
A: Idealnya ada dedicated Content Lead, but initial phase bisa PM + Designer collaborate. See [04-Workflow-Checklist](./04-workflow-checklist.md) untuk role breakdown.

**Q: Berapa budget yang dibutuhkan untuk tools?**  
A: Minimal setup: ~$50-100/bulan (Canva Pro + Buffer). Full setup: ~$200-300/bulan. See [05-Tools-Resources](./05-tools-resources.md).
