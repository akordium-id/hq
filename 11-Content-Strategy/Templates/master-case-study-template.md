## ðŸ“‹ Usage Guidelines

### How to Use This Template
1. **Duplicate this template** for each case study
2. **Fill all sections** - be thorough and specific
3. **Gather visual assets** as you work through the project
4. **Collect quotes** in real-time, don't try to recall later
5. **Track metrics** from the beginning, not just at the end

**High-Value Sections for Content Creation:**

- **Results & Impact:** Great for social media posts and marketing materials
- **Lessons Learned:** Excellent for thought leadership content
- **Technical Challenges:** Ideal for blog posts and tutorials
- **Quotes Section:** Ready-to-use content for all platforms

A: Anonymize client details, use ranges instead of exact numbers, or focus on technical/process insights rather than business metrics.
