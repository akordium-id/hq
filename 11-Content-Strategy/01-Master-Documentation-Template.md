2. **Fill all sections** - be thorough and specific
3. **Gather visual assets** as you work through the project
4. **Collect quotes** in real-time, don't try to recall later
5. **Track metrics** from the beginning, not just at the end

- **Total:** 15-20 hours across team

**Q: How detailed should each section be?**
A: Aim for 2-3 paragraphs minimum per subsection. Include specific examples, metrics, and quotes wherever possible.

**Q: What if some information is sensitive?**
A: Anonymize client details, use ranges instead of exact numbers, or focus on technical/process insights rather than business metrics.

**Q: How often should we update this?**
A: For completed projects, review quarterly. For ongoing projects, update at major milestones or when significant results are achieved.

**Q: Who is responsible for maintaining this?**
A: Project Manager owns the document, but all team members contribute to their respective sections.
