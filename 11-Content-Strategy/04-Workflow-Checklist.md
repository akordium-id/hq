### üîç Phase 1: Project Selection & Setup (Week 1)

#### Day 1-2: Project Identification & Evaluation
**Owner:** Project Manager + Content Lead
**Time:** 3-4 hours total

**Checklist:**
- [ ] **Project Selection Criteria Review:**
  - [ ] Measurable business impact and clear results
  - [ ] Interesting technical or design challenges
  - [ ] Client approval for publication (or anonymization possible)
  - [ ] Sufficient documentation and assets available
  - [ ] Storytelling potential and emotional hook

- [ ] **Initial Project Assessment:**
  - [ ] Gather basic project information (timeline, budget, team)
  - [ ] Identify key metrics and results achieved
  - [ ] Note unique challenges and innovative solutions
  - [ ] Assess visual content availability (screenshots, designs)
  - [ ] Check for client testimonials and feedback

- [ ] **Go/No-Go Decision:**
  - [ ] Score project against selection criteria (1-10 scale)
  - [ ] Confirm minimum score of 7/10 for selection
  - [ ] Identify any potential blockers or risks
  - [ ] Finalize project selection for case study

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Project selection decision document
- [ ] Initial project summary (1-2 pages)
- [ ] Risk assessment and mitigation plan

#### Day 3-4: Client Approval & Legal Review
**Owner:** Project Manager
**Time:** 2-3 hours

**Checklist:**
- [ ] **Client Communication:**
  - [ ] Draft client approval request email
  - [ ] Include clear benefits of publication for client
  - [ ] Specify what information can/cannot be shared
  - [ ] Offer anonymization options if needed
  - [ ] Set timeline for client response

- [ ] **Legal & Compliance Review:**
  - [ ] Review NDA and confidentiality agreements
  - [ ] Check intellectual property ownership clauses
  - [ ] Verify data privacy compliance requirements
  - [ ] Identify sensitive information that needs anonymization
  - [ ] Document approval process and requirements

- [ ] **Internal Approvals:**
  - [ ] Secure internal team approvals for publication
  - [ ] Confirm resource allocation for content creation
  - [ ] Align on timeline and deliverables
  - [ ] Establish quality standards and approval workflow

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Client approval documentation
- [ ] Legal compliance checklist
- [ ] Internal approval sign-offs

#### Day 5: Team Assignment & Kickoff
**Owner:** Content Lead
**Time:** 1-2 hours

**Checklist:**
- [ ] **Team Role Assignment:**
  - [ ] Assign Content Lead with overall responsibility
  - [ ] Assign Project Manager for client coordination
  - [ ] Assign Designer for visual content creation
  - [ ] Assign Developer for technical content
  - [ ] Assign Video Editor for video content
  - [ ] Assign Social Media Manager for distribution

- [ ] **Project Kickoff Meeting:**
  - [ ] Schedule and conduct kickoff meeting (60 minutes)
  - [ ] Present project overview and goals

  - [ ] Clarify roles and responsibilities
  - [ ] Establish communication protocols

- [ ] **Tools & Access Setup:**

## üõ†Ô∏è Quality Control Checklist

### Content Quality Standards
**Written Content Quality:**
- [ ] Content accuracy verified by subject matter experts
- [ ] Grammar and spelling proofread by multiple reviewers
- [ ] Consistent brand voice and tone across all platforms
- [ ] SEO optimization with appropriate keywords and metadata
- [ ] Clear calls-to-action and next steps for readers

**Visual Content Quality:**
- [ ] Brand consistency with approved color palette and fonts
- [ ] High-resolution images optimized for each platform
- [ ] Professional design with clear information hierarchy
- [ ] Accessibility compliance with alt text and captions
- [ ] Mobile-optimized formatting and layout

**Video Content Quality:**
- [ ] Clear audio quality with proper volume levels
- [ ] Professional video quality with stable framing
- [ ] Consistent branding with intros, outros, and lower thirds
- [ ] Closed captions or subtitles for accessibility
- [ ] Appropriate length and pacing for target platform

### Approval Workflow
**Review Process:**
1. **Creator Review:** Content creator reviews for completeness and accuracy
2. **Peer Review:** Team member reviews for quality and consistency
3. **Subject Matter Expert Review:** Technical/design expert verifies accuracy
4. **Brand Review:** Marketing/brand team ensures compliance
5. **Legal/Compliance Review:** Sensitive content reviewed for compliance
6. **Final Approval:** Content Lead gives final sign-off for publication

**Approval Tracking:**
- [ ] All review stages documented with dates and reviewers
- [ ] Feedback and revision history maintained
- [ ] Final approval sign-offs recorded
- [ ] Publication authorization documented

## üö® Troubleshooting Guide

### Common Issues and Solutions

**Low Engagement Rates:**
- **Problem:** Content not generating expected engagement
- **Diagnosis:** Review posting times, content relevance, call-to-action clarity
- **Solution:** A/B test headlines, adjust posting schedule, improve visual quality

**Technical Upload Issues:**
- **Problem:** Content fails to upload or display properly
- **Diagnosis:** Check file formats, sizes, platform specifications
- **Solution:** Reformat files, reduce file sizes, test on different devices

**Negative Feedback or Comments:**
- **Problem:** Receiving criticism or negative responses
- **Diagnosis:** Assess if feedback is constructive or trolling
- **Solution:** Respond professionally to constructive feedback, ignore/report trolls

**Platform Algorithm Changes:**
- **Problem:** Sudden drop in reach or engagement
- **Diagnosis:** Research recent platform updates and algorithm changes
- **Solution:** Adapt content strategy to new algorithm preferences

**Resource Constraints:**
- **Problem:** Insufficient time or budget for content creation
- **Diagnosis:** Review resource allocation and efficiency
- **Solution:** Prioritize high-impact content, automate where possible, consider outsourcing

### Emergency Response Plan
**Content Removal:**
- [ ] Identify content that needs immediate removal
- [ ] Document reason for removal and lessons learned
- [ ] Communicate with stakeholders about the situation
- [ ] Plan content replacement if needed

**Public Relations Issues:**
- [ ] Monitor for potential PR concerns from content
- [ ] Prepare response statements in advance
- [ ] Escalate to leadership for serious issues
- [ ] Document lessons learned and update processes

**Platform Account Issues:**
- [ ] Monitor account security and access
- [ ] Have backup communication channels ready
- [ ] Document all platform contact information
- [ ] Regular backup of content and analytics data
