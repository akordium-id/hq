## ğŸ¯ Market Positioning Strategy

### Positioning Statement

"Untuk startup dan SaaS Indonesia yang membutuhkan sistem feedback management yang simple dan affordable, Katauser adalah platform feedback management berbahasa Indonesia dengan integrasi Telegram Bot dan harga 30-40% lebih murah dari UserJot, memungkinkan Anda mengelola feedback user dengan efisien tanpa language barrier."

### Target Segments

**Primary Segment (70% focus):**
- Indonesian SaaS companies (early to growth stage)
- Series A to B startups
- 100-10,000 users
- Tech-savvy founders
- Willing to pay IDR 200K-500K/month

**Secondary Segment (20% focus):**
- Digital agencies dengan multiple client projects
- Need white-label solutions
- Higher budget (IDR 500K-1M/month)

**Tertiary Segment (10% focus):**
- Non-SaaS startups (marketplace, e-commerce, edtech)
- Simpler feedback needs
- Price-sensitive

### Competitive Advantages

1. **Localization Advantage**
   - Full Bahasa Indonesia UI/UX
   - Indonesian customer support
   - Localized documentation dan tutorials
   - Cultural relevance (Bahasa yang natural, bukan kaku)

2. **Integration Advantage**
   - Telegram Bot (favorite channel komunitas Indo)
   - Indonesian payment gateways (GoPay, OVO, Dana)
   - Local email services (Ruangguru, etc.)

3. **Pricing Advantage**
   - 30-40% cheaper than UserJot
   - Pricing dalam IDR (no currency conversion confusion)
   - Flexible payment (monthly/yearly with 20% discount)
   - Student/startup discounts (case-by-case)

4. **Community Advantage**
   - Focus on Indonesian startup ecosystem
   - Community-building via Telegram groups
   - Local events and meetups sponsorship
   - Partnership dengan startup communities (Indonesian Startups, etc.)
