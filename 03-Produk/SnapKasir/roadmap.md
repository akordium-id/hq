## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High-Risk Areas

1. **Market Competition**
   - Risk: Large tech companies entering POS space
   - Mitigation: Strong local partnerships, differentiated features

2. **Payment Processing Risks**
   - Risk: Payment gateway failures, regulation changes
   - Mitigation: Multiple gateway partners, offline capability

3. **Hardware Compatibility**
   - Risk: Integration issues dengan diverse hardware
   - Mitigation: Extensive testing, certified hardware partners

### Medium-Risk Areas

1. **User Adoption**
   - Risk: Low technology adoption di traditional UMKM
   - Mitigation: Simplified UI, extensive training, support

2. **Data Security**
   - Risk: Payment data breaches, compliance issues
   - Mitigation: PCI DSS compliance, regular security audits

### Contingency Plans

- **Market Entry Competition:** Focus pada enterprise clients, differentiation strategy
- **Technology Disruption:** Cloud-based architecture, rapid iteration capability
- **Regulatory Changes:** Compliance-first approach, legal partnership
