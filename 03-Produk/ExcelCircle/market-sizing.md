## Objective

Calculate TAM/SAM/SOM untuk ExcelCircle di Indonesia market dengan fokus pada:
- Office workers (admin, HR, finance)
- Data analysts & business intelligence professionals
- Mahasiswa (analisis data tugas kuliah, skripsi)
- UMKM owners (manage keuangan, inventory, laporan)

## TAM (Total Addressable Market)

### Indonesia Population & Workforce

- **Total Population:** 270+ million (2024)
- **Workforce Age (15-64):** ~180 million
- **Office Workers:** ~15-20 million

### Excel/Google Sheets Usage

**Estimates:**
- Office workers yang gunakan Excel/Sheets daily: ~60% (9-12 million)
- Data analysts & BI professionals: ~500K
- Mahasiswa aktif: ~3-4 million (30-40% gunakan Excel)
- UMKM owners: ~5-6 million (20-30% gunakan Excel)

**TAM Calculation:** ~15-18 million users

## SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)

### Geographic Focus

**Initial Target:** Major urban areas (Jakarta, Surabaya, Bandung, Medan, Makassar)
- Urban office workers: ~50% of TAM
- **SAM:** ~7-9 million users

### Accessible via Digital Channels

- Internet penetration di urban areas: ~75%
- Comfortable dengan SaaS tools: ~50%
- **SAM Refined:** ~2.5-3.5 million users

## SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)

### Realistic Penetration (Year 1-3)

**Conservative Estimate:** 1-2% of SAM
- Year 1 target: 25K-70K users
- Year 2 target: 50K-140K users
- Year 3 target: 75K-210K users

**Aggressive Estimate:** 3-5% of SAM
- Year 1 target: 75K-175K users
- Year 2 target: 150K-350K users
- Year 3 target: 225K-525K users

## Market Segments Breakdown

### Primary: Office Workers (60%)

**Admin & HR:**
- Monthly active Excel users: ~5-7 million
- Pain points: Payroll, attendance, leave calculations
- Willingness to pay: Rp 20-50K/month

**Finance & Accounting:**
- Monthly active Excel users: ~2-3 million
- Pain points: Financial reports, budgeting, tax calculations
- Willingness to pay: Rp 30-100K/month

### Secondary: Data Analysts (15%)

**BI & Analytics Professionals:**
- Monthly active Excel users: ~300-500K
- Pain points: Complex formulas, data transformation, pivot tables
- Willingness to pay: Rp 50-150K/month

### Tertiary: Students (15%)

**University Students:**
- Monthly active Excel users: ~1-2 million
- Pain points: Thesis analysis, assignments, statistics
- Willingness to pay: Rp 10-30K/month

### Quaternary: UMKM Owners (10%)

**Small Business Owners:**
- Monthly active Excel users: ~500K-1M
- Pain points: Invoicing, inventory, financial reports
- Willingness to pay: Rp 20-60K/month
