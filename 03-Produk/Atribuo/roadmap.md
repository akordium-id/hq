## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High-Risk Areas

1. **Market Risk**
   - Risk: Low market demand untuk skill gamification
   - Mitigation: Extensive market validation sebelum development

2. **Technical Risk**
   - Risk: Complex real-time visualization performance
   - Mitigation: Incremental development dengan performance testing

3. **Competition Risk**
   - Risk: Big players entering gamification space
   - Mitigation: Strong community building dan unique differentiation

### Medium-Risk Areas

1. **Retention Risk**
   - Risk: Users lose interest setelah initial novelty
   - Mitigation: Continuous feature updates dan community engagement

2. **Monetization Risk**
   - Risk: Low conversion rate ke premium
   - Mitigation: A/B testing pricing models dan value proposition

### Contingency Plans

- **Pivot Strategy:** Focus B2B model jika B2C conversion low
- **Feature Reduction:** Cut complex features untuk faster time-to-market
- **Partnership Acceleration:** Accelerate partnerships jika organic growth slow
