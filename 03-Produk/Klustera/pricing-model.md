## üéØ Pricing Philosophy

**Principles:**
1. **Transparent Pricing:** No hidden fees, published pricing for all tiers
2. **Value-Based:** Price based on value delivered (time savings, efficiency gains)
3. **Competitive:** 50-70% cheaper than competitors
4. **Fair:** Pay per unit (not per user), unlimited resident accounts included
5. **Flexible:** Monthly billing, annual discounts, easy cancellation

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Replace 1 admin staff (Rp 3-5M/month) dengan platform (Rp 1-1.5M/month)
- Save 8 hours/week on administrative tasks
- Improve collection rate dari 65% ‚Üí 90%+
- Reduce conflicts melalui transparency

- Rp 43.2M / Rp 5M = **8.6:1**
- **SaaS Benchmark:** 3:1 is considered healthy
- **Klustera:** 8.6:1 is excellent! ‚úÖ

**Payback Period:**
- CAC: Rp 5M

## üéÅ Discount Strategies

### Volume Discounts

**Multi-Complex Discount:** 10% off untuk 3+ complexes
- **Target:** Property developers, management companies
- **Example:** 5 complexes @ 300 units each
  - Regular: 5 √ó 300 √ó Rp 4,000 = Rp 6M/month
  - Discounted: Rp 6M √ó 0.9 = **Rp 5.4M/month** (save Rp 720K/month)

- 50-200 units: Rp 5,000/unit

**Standard:** 2 months free (17% discount)
- **Mechanics:** Pay 12 months upfront, get 14 months of service
- **Example:** 300-unit complex
  - Monthly: Rp 1.2M √ó 12 = Rp 14.4M

- **Typical:** 2-3 months free (15-20% discount)
- **Commitment:** 12-month minimum

### Non-Profit / RT-RW Discount

**Eligibility:**
- Registered RT/RW (verified via KTP & Surat Keterangan dari Kelurahan)

## üí° Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring

**Strategy:** Show highest price first to make other prices seem reasonable

**Implementation:**
- Pricing page shows: Enterprise (Rp 25M) ‚Üí Standard (Rp 1.2M) ‚Üí Community (Free)
- Standard seems "cheap" compared to Enterprise

### Decoy Effect

**Strategy:** Add less attractive option to make preferred option shine

**Implementation:**
- **Option A:** Community (Free, 50 units, basic features)
- **Option B:** Standard Monthly (Rp 1.2M, unlimited, all features) ‚Üê **Preferred**
- **Option C:** Standard Annual (Rp 11.96M, but "pay 12 get 14") ‚Üê **Decoy**
- Result: Monthly looks expensive compared to Annual, so customers choose Annual

### Loss Aversion

**Strategy:** Emphasize what customer loses by not using Klustera

**Messaging:**
- "Without Klustera, you're losing Rp 540K/month due to 65% collection rate (vs 90% dengan Klustera)"
- "Without Klustera, you're spending 10 hours/week on admin tasks (vs 2 hours dengan Klustera)"

### Social Proof

**Strategy:** Show testimonials from similar complexes

**Implementation:**
- "Kompleks Graha Indah (300 units) increased collection rate dari 65% ‚Üí 92% in 3 months"
- "Permata Housing saved Rp 4M/month by reducing admin staff dari 2 ‚Üí 0"

## üéØ Pricing Governance

### Review Cadence

- **Quarterly:** Review pricing, analyze competitors, assess market response
- **Annually:** Major pricing adjustments (inflation, new features)
- **On-Demand:** Special pricing untuk strategic accounts (negotiated)

### Approval Process

- **Standard Pricing:** Approved by Product Lead
- **Enterprise Deals:** Approved by CEO/Founder
- **Discounts > 20%:** Requires CEO approval

### Price Changes

**Grandfathering:**
- Existing customers: Keep old pricing untuk 12 months after price change
- New customers: New pricing applies immediately

**Communication:**
- Notify customers 60 days before price increase
- Explain reasons (inflation, new features, etc.)
- Offer option to lock in old pricing dengan 2-year commitment
