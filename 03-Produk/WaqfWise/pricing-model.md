**1. Market Penetration:**
- Free tier allows masjid kecil dan nazhir perorangan to adopt

**4. Social Impact:**
- Free tier supports digitalisasi wakf di semua economic levels

- Builds goodwill dalam waqf community

## ğŸ’¡ Pricing FAQ

### Common Customer Questions

**Q: Apakah harga include payment gateway fees?**
A: Tidak. Payment gateway fees (Midtrans, BSI, BNI Syariah) ditanggung oleh end user (Wakif/donors), bukan yayasan nazhir.

**Q: Apakah ada biaya setup atau implementation?**
A: Tidak ada biaya setup. Kami bantu data migration dan training secara gratis untuk Premium customers.

**Q: Apakah ada commit minimum (e.g., 1-year contract)?**
A: Tidak ada. Month-to-month payment, cancel anytime. Tapi kami sarankan annual billing untuk 17% discount.

**Q: Apa yang terjadi jika jumlah assets melebihi limit?**
A: System akan notify dan memberikan option untuk upgrade plan atau remove old assets. Grace period 30 days sebelum automatic upgrade.

**Q: Apakah ada additional fee untuk blockchain certificates?**
A: Rp 50,000 per certificate untuk gas fee recovery. Medium dan Large plans include 100 certificates/month free.

**Q: Apakah support included dalam pricing?**
A: Yes. Email support (response <24 hours) included. Priority support (WhatsApp/Phone) available untuk Medium dan Large plans.

**Q: Apakah bisa self-hosting?**
A: Ya, untuk Enterprise plan dengan custom pricing. Minimum 3-year commitment required.

**Q: Apakah bisa downgrade plan?**
A: Ya, bisa downgrade anytime dengan efek di bulan berikutnya. Tidak ada refund untuk bulan berjalan.

**Q: Apakah ada free trial untuk Premium plans?**
A: Ya, 30-day free trial untuk semua Premium plans. No credit card required.

## ğŸ¯ Pricing Success Metrics

### Key Metrics untuk Track

**Acquisition Metrics:**
- Free tier registration rate - Target: 500+ users Year 1
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate - Target: >20%
- Free-to-paid upsell rate - Target: >10% per year
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) - Target: < Rp 1M per customer

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Average revenue per customer (ARPC) - Target: Rp 1.5M/month (Year 1)
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) growth - Target: >20% MoM (Year 1)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV) - Target: > Rp 15M (2+ tahun retention)
- Annual recurring revenue (ARR) - Target: Rp 1.86M (Year 1)

**Retention Metrics:**
- Monthly churn rate - Target: <2% per month
- Annual retention rate - Target: >80%
- Reasons untuk churn (track untuk improvement)

### Pricing Optimization Triggers

**Increase Pricing jika:**
- Churn rate <1% (very low churn = could increase price)
- Trial-to-paid conversion >30% (high demand = inelastic demand)
- Competitors enter market dengan higher prices

**Decrease Pricing jika:**
- Churn rate >5% (high churn = price too high)
- Trial-to-paid conversion <10% (low conversion = price sensitive)
- Competitors significantly undercut (market pressure)

**Introduce New Tier jika:**
- Gap antara Small dan Medium terlalu besar (user feedback)
- Enterprise demand increases (need tier di atas Large)
- Micro-nazhir segment underserved (need tier di bawah Small)
