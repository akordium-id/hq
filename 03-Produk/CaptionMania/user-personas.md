|-------|---------|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **1. Preparation** | Takes product photos | Need good lighting | Photography takes time | AI photo enhancement? |
| **2. Content Creation** | Writes title & description | What keywords to use? | SEO research is tedious | Auto-suggest keywords |
| **3. Listing** | Fills in marketplace form | This is repetitive | Copy-paste is manual | Bulk generation |
| **4. Optimization** | Edits for better SEO | Is this good enough? | Uncertainty about quality | A/B testing suggestions |
| **5. Publishing** | Goes live | Hope it sells | Anxious about performance | Analytics dashboard |

- [ ] Tokopedia/Shopee seller Facebook groups
