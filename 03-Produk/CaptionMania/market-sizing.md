## SAM: Serviceable Addressable Market

### Definition: Sellers within TAM that CaptionMania can realistically serve

#### Segmentation Criteria

**1. Listing Frequency**
- Sellers who list products regularly (at least monthly)
- Active content creators vs passive sellers

**2. Tech Awareness**
- Sellers aware of AI tools/digital solutions
- Comfortable with SaaS products
- Have basic tech literacy

**3. Business Size**
- Exclude enterprise sellers (may build in-house)
- Focus on SME, power sellers, individual entrepreneurs

**4. Geography**
- Java + major cities initially (better internet, higher e-commerce adoption)
- Nationwide expansion in Year 2+
