## Value-Based Pricing Analysis

### Value Proposition Calculator

**Current State (Without CaptionMania):**
- Time per caption: 15-30 minutes
- Seller hourly rate: Rp 100.000 - 500.000
- Cost per caption: Rp 25.000 - 250.000
- Monthly caption volume: 20-200 captions
- **Monthly cost: Rp 500.000 - 50.000.000**

**With CaptionMania:**
- Time per caption: 1-2 minutes
- Monthly subscription: Rp 99.000
- **Monthly cost: Rp 99.000 + seller time**

**ROI:**
- Breakeven: ~4-200 captions/month depending on seller rate
- **For power sellers (50+ captions/month): 5-50x ROI**

**Value Communication:**
*"Generate 50 captions/month for Rp 99.000 instead of spending Rp 1.250.000+ of your time"*

## Price Testing Strategy

### A/B Testing Approach

**Pre-Launch:**
1. **Landing Page Variant Pricing**
   - Variant A: Rp 99k/month only
   - Variant B: Rp 99k/month + annual option highlighted
   - Measure: Email signup conversion

2. **Survey Pricing Cards**
   - Test 3 price points: Rp 79k, Rp 99k, Rp 149k
   - Measure: Purchase intent at each price

3. **Conjoint Analysis**
   - Trade-off studies: price vs features
   - Identify must-have vs nice-to-have features

**Post-Launch:**
- Monitor conversion rate by traffic source
- Track churn by user segment
- Test annual discount (10%, 15%, 20%)
