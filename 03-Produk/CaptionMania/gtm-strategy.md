## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)

**Goal:** Build waitlist of 300-500 potential users

**Tactics:**

**1. Landing Page (Week 1)**
- [ ] Create simple, focused landing page
- [ ] Clear value proposition headline
- [ ] Email signup form (waitlist)
- [ ] Product demo video (60-90 seconds)
- [ ] Countdown to launch

**2. Content Marketing (Weeks 1-4)**
- [ ] Publish 4-6 blog posts:
  - "5 Cara Meningkatkan Penjualan Tokopedia dengan Caption SEO-Friendly"

- [ ] Join 10-15 Tokopedia/Shopee seller Facebook groups
- [ ] Provide value first (answer questions, share tips)

- Build personal brand as marketplace expert
- Soft product mentions when relevant
- Host free training sessions

**Expected CAC:** Rp 50k - Rp 100k
**Conversion Rate:** 5-10%
**Timeline:** Immediate impact, compounding over time

#### 2. Content Marketing + SEO (25%)

**Strategy:** Rank for high-intent keywords, build authority

**Tactics:**
- Blog posts (2-3 per week)
- YouTube tutorials (caption tips, product demos)

## Risk Mitigation

### Launch Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low waitlist signups | Medium | High | Double down on community engagement |
| Poor product-market fit | Low | Critical | Beta phase will surface this early |
| High churn after launch | Medium | High | Focus on success metrics & onboarding |
| Competitor launches first | Low | Medium | Speed to market, differentiation |
| Negative reviews on launch | Low | Medium | extensive beta testing, responsive support |

### Contingency Plans

**If waitlist <100 after 4 weeks:**
- Increase content production
- Paid ads (Rp 1M-2M) to landing page
- Direct outreach to sellers

**If beta retention <60%:**
- Deep user interviews
- Product iteration
- Delay public launch until PMF improved

**If public launch <20 users in first month:**
- Revisit messaging
- More aggressive promotional pricing
- Intensify influencer partnerships
