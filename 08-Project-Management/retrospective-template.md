## ğŸ› ï¸ Retrospective Tools & Resources

### **Physical Tools:**

- **Whiteboard or flip chart** with markers
- **Sticky notes** in multiple colors
- **Dot voting** materials (dots or stickers)
- **Timer** for timeboxing
- **Camera** for capturing boards

### **Digital Tools:**

- **Miro/Mural** for virtual retrospectives
- **FunRetro** for online retrospective facilitation
- **Confluence/Notion** for documentation
- **Jira/Trello** for action tracking
- **Zoom/Teams** for virtual meetings

### **Icebreakers & Team Building:**

- **One-word check-in:** One word about how you're feeling
- **Temperature check:** Rate the sprint on 1-5 scale
- **Rose, Bud, Thorn:** Rose (positive), Bud (potential), Thorn (challenge)
- **Sailboat exercise:** Identify winds, anchors, rocks, and destination

### **Facilitation Tips:**

#### **For Facilitators:**

- **Create psychological safety** through ground rules
- **Use timeboxing** to keep discussions focused
- **Encourage participation** from all team members
- **Stay neutral** and avoid leading the discussion
- **Document thoroughly** for future reference

#### **For Participants:**

- **Be honest but constructive** in feedback
- **Focus on processes, not people**
- **Come prepared** with thoughts and examples
- **Listen actively** to others' perspectives
- **Commit to action items** you own
